Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TapCoin Empire ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you play.

1. Information We Collect

TapCoin Empire works without collecting personal information. Your progress — coins, upgrades, levels — is stored locally on your device and is never sent to our servers.

2. Advertising

If the App shows ads (for example through Google AdMob) to keep gameplay free, our ad partners may automatically collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. This is handled under Google's own privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

3. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has shared personal information with us, contact us and we'll remove it.

4. Data Security

Since your gameplay data stays on your device, you're in control of it — clearing app storage or uninstalling the App removes it completely.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will appear on this page with a new revision date.

6. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Email youremail@gmail.com.
